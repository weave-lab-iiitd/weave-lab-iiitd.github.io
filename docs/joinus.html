About

At Weave, we are an interdisciplinary group of research maniacs who love to ideate, prototype, develop, evaluate and then CELEBRATE. We aim to become one of the most innovative tinkering labs in the world and are always looking for talented and motivated applicants to join us.

We welcome people from different backgrounds and give them the freedom to design innovative solutions to real-world problems. If you feel you have that edge, find the perfect position on this page!

Application Process

  1. Reviewing CV, SOP and Portfolio
  2. Interview with head and team lead
  3. Interview with faculty
